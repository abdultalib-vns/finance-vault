# design.md

## Project

Design a world-class landing page for **FinAura**, a premium AI-powered Personal Finance Management platform.

The visual quality should feel like a combination of Apple, Stripe, Linear, Arc Browser, Vercel, and Notion—not a typical fintech template.

The website must communicate luxury, trust, intelligence, simplicity, and engineering excellence.

Avoid anything that feels like a crypto website, banking portal, SaaS template, or dashboard marketplace.

---

# Brand Personality

FinAura is the future operating system for personal wealth.

It helps users understand, optimize, automate, and grow their finances using AI.

The brand should feel:

* Intelligent
* Premium
* Minimal
* Timeless
* Calm
* Precise
* Confident
* Trustworthy

Never playful.

Never colorful.

Never noisy.

---

# Design Language

Follow Apple's Human Interface philosophy.

Everything should feel intentional.

Every spacing, radius, shadow, animation and typography should look professionally art directed.

Think:

* Stripe
* Linear
* Raycast
* Ramp
* Apple
* Vercel
* OpenAI
* Arc Browser

Do NOT imitate.

Capture the quality.
